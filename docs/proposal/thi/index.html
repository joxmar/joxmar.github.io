1. Strategy (Content Strategy + Wireframes)

We'll begin with a full audit of your existing website content. This includes analyzing your current 20+ pages, assessing content quality, and developing a new sitemap that aligns with your goals. Wireframes will then be created for the homepage, blog, and key components to map out user experience before design begins.

Content Strategy & Information Architecture ~20 hours

  • Navigation/sitemap restructuring
  • Recommendations for content trimming, merging, and rewriting

2. Wireframes (UX Design) ~18 hours

  • Homepage wireframe
  • Key layout patterns for reusable blocks

2. Visual Design

I will present two homepage concepts based on the new brand guidelines you'll receive from your brand agency. Once we finalize a direction, I’ll design templates for the homepage, product page, blog post, and create visuals for all reusable block components.

Visual Design ~32 hours

I’ll also create 15 reusable Gutenberg blocks tailored to your content strategy, configure transactional registration flows, forms, and integrate Constant Contact along with any additional services your site requires.

  • WordPress, WooCommerce, ACF, plugin setup

The Fine Print

To ensure the success of this project and keep everything running smoothly, here are a few important details and expectations:

Client Responsibilities

To begin and progress the work efficiently, the client agrees to provide:
  • All necessary logins and access credentials (e.g., current website, hosting, domain registrar, Constant Contact, etc.)
  • Final or in-progress content (text, videos, photography, downloadable files)
  • Information or files related to any gated or password-protected content
  • Brand assets (logos, fonts, color palettes) once branding is complete
  • Delays in providing these materials or completing the brand identity will result in corresponding delays to the project timeline.

Design Approval & Edits

You will receive two homepage design concepts. Once a direction is selected, you’ll receive one round of revisions to refine it. Additional revisions or change requests outside the agreed scope will be billed at $50/hour.

Payment Schedule
The project total of $11,000 will be divided into three payments:
  • $4,000 — Due upon project start (deposit)
  • $3,500 — Due at the midpoint (after design approval, before development begins)
  • $3,500 — Due at project launch

Maintenance & Support

  • The project includes a 3-month post-launch warranty, which covers technical support for theme-related bugs and integration issues, and periodic site testing.
  • Content edits, new features, or non-bug-related changes are not included in this warranty but can be covered under a monthly retainer if desired.

Optional Monthly Maintenance Retainer
  • Retainers include a fixed number of hours per month for support, updates, and minor changes.
  • Unused hours expire at the end of each month, with a maximum rollover of up to 50% of the previous month’s hours, valid for one additional month. After that, they expire.
  • Retainer plans do not include large-scale feature development, rebranding, or major restructuring.
